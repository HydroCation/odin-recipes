Vegetarian Stir Fry

Stir Fried Vegetables

What's not to love about a fresh bunch of sauteed vegetables? Coated in a
creamy and savoury sauce, this dish will satiate your tastebuds and won't even be too
hard on the wallet!

Delicious on its own, or with a serving of rice or noodles, this recipe will
keep you coming back for more. Not to mention, it's terribly healthy too!

  • 1 large sliced carrot
  • 2 cups of medium broccoli florets
  • 200 grams of baby corn spears
  • 200 grams of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 whole sliced and deseeded pepper
  • 2 Tbsp cooking oil
  • 3 peeled and minced cloves of garlic
  • 2 tsp minced ginger

Steps To Prepare

  1. In a large non-stick wok, heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the vegetables and stir fry for 3 minutes or until crisp-tender.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger and cook until fragrant.
