Paneer Butter Masala

Tender cubes of paneer enveloped in a spicy tomato gravy, this culinary delight
is a feast for the senses and is also extremely simple to just whip up in your
own kitchen!

Whether paired with warm flatbreads or fragrant rice, this dish satisfies
everyone's cravings for great Indian cuisine. Best of all, it's vegetarian!

  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1 cup cubed onions
  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 250 grams diced paneer
  • 1 tsp dried fenugreek leaves
  • 3 Tbsp cream
  • Coriander powder

Steps To Prepare

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add green cardamoms and onions. Stir fry until transparent.
  2. Add tomatoes and salt. Saute for 3 minutes. Cover and cook until mushy and soft.
  3. Add garam masala, coriander powder and red chilli powder.
  4. Stir fry till the mix turns aromatic. Then cool completely.
  5. Transfer to a blender, add water and puree to a silky consistency.
  6. Heat the puree to a rolling boil, add paneer and fenugreek leaves.
  7. Mix, cook for 2 mintes, pour cream, stir and serve.
