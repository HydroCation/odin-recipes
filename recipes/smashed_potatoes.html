Smashed Roasted Potatoes

Buttery, crispy smashed potatoes. What's not to love? They're crispy as anything on
the outside and as fluffy as a cloud on the inside. With potatoes like these, you wouldn't
want to eat anything else anyway!

  • 700 grams small potatoes
  • 1.2 kg medium potatoes
  • 30 grams butter
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps To Prepare

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil and add salt. Cook potatoes until soft.
  2. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C. Place potatoes on tray and squish them with a potato masher.
  3. Drizzle with butter, olive oil, salt and pepper.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes or until deep golden and crispy. Serve hot.
